üî¨ Technical Insights

Algorithm Performance

Compared multiple clustering approaches to ensure optimal segment quality:

  • K-means: Selected for interpretability and computational efficiency
  • DBSCAN: Tested for noise handling but less interpretable for business teams
  • Gaussian Mixture Models: Provided probabilistic assignments but slower performance
  • Hierarchical Clustering: Useful for understanding segment relationships

Model Validation

Ensured segment quality through multiple validation techniques:

  • Silhouette score of 0.72 indicating well-separated clusters
  • Business validation with marketing team for segment interpretability
  • A/B testing of segment-based campaigns for performance validation
  • Regular model retraining to adapt to changing customer behaviors

üéì Lessons Learned

This project provided valuable insights for successful customer segmentation implementations:

  • Business alignment is crucial: Segments must be actionable and understandable by marketing teams
  • Data quality drives success: Comprehensive data integration is essential for accurate segmentation
  • Dynamic segmentation matters: Customer behavior changes, so segments must be updated regularly
  • Cross-functional collaboration: Close work with marketing ensured practical implementation
  • Measurement framework: Clear KPIs for each segment enabled performance tracking
  • Technology integration: Seamless connection with marketing platforms drove adoption
