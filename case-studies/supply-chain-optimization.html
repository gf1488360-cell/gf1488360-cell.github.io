üéØ Business Challenge

The client, a Fortune 500 manufacturing company with $2.1B in annual revenue, faced critical supply chain challenges across their global operations:

  • 45% of revenue spent on logistics and transportation costs
  • 68% on-time delivery rate causing customer dissatisfaction and penalty fees
  • 28% excess inventory carrying costs across 15 warehouses
  • Fragmented planning with 12 different regional systems
  • 35% vehicle utilization rate with empty backhauls
  • Manual route planning taking 3+ days for monthly allocations
  • Inability to respond to supply disruptions and demand fluctuations
  • Carbon emissions 40% above industry benchmarks

The existing systems relied on Excel-based planning and experienced planners' intuition, lacking mathematical optimization capabilities.
